The Cleaning Company

Established in 2010, The Cleaning Company has become one of the most successful, dynamic and professional cleaning company in London. We offer an unbeatable service, reliability and levels of customer care that have set an industry standard. We provide quality commercial and domestic cleaning services to all Central and Greater London areas including Home Counties. The cleaning company aims to introduce only dependable, honest and professional cleaners to our clients.

The services we offer:

Kitchen
cleaning

We perform cleaning, washing, sweeping, mopping, dusting, washing up, polishing and vacuum cleaning of the kitchen including an overall clean of the oven, microwave and ny other devices on request to the required standard.

General room
cleaning

This includes dusting, vacuuming, sweeping and mopping the floors in all rooms. Cleaning the bathrooms, including mirrors, toilets, showers and baths.

Carpet
cleaning

We perform pre-cleaning inspections, apply treatments, and remove stains using hand and machine cleaning tools.

Window
